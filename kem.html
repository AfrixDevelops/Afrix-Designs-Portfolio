Kamloops Electric Motor

An electric motor and generator company that is seeking to expand its business facilities and services. This project will involve discovering and establishing the company’s brand, including the design of a new logo and other print materials.

About the logo

This logo deconstructs the ‘K’ letterform and rebuilds it using an electric bolt to form the vertical potion of the letter. The logo is paired with a heavy San Serif typeface that balances the logos energetic motif.
